01 — PROGRESSION

The only rule
that matters.

The Double Progression Rule

Hit the top of your rep range on all sets? Add 2–5 kg next session, drop to the bottom of the range. Repeat forever. This is the entire mechanism of muscle growth.

Track every set — weight and reps. Every session. No exceptions.

02 — EFFORT (RPE)

How hard
to push.

  • RPE 7 — 3 reps left in tank (minimum)
  • RPE 8 — 2 reps left (target)
  • RPE 9 — 1 rep left (occasionally fine)
  • RPE 10 — absolute failure (avoid)

Stop 1–3 reps short of failure. Keeps form clean and fatigue manageable.

03 — NUTRITION

Fuel the
work.

  • Protein: bodyweight (kg) × 1.8–2 g / day
  • 75 kg → ~140 g protein daily
  • Eat enough — no big deficit while building
  • Protein with every meal
  • Eat within 2 hrs after training

Sources: chicken, eggs, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, fish.

04 — RECOVERY

Where growth
actually happens.

  • Sleep 7–9 hrs — non-negotiable
  • 90% of muscle synthesis happens during deep sleep
  • 2–3 litres of water daily
  • Chronic stress directly inhibits muscle growth
  • Sunday is a real rest day — no heavy cardio

Going too
heavy

Form breaks down, injuries follow. Control every rep. Ego is the enemy.

Skipping
legs

Legs are half your body and trigger whole-body anabolic hormones. Never skip.

Not sleeping
enough

Sleep is the most powerful muscle-building tool you have. 7 hours minimum.

Changing
programs

Stick to this for 12 weeks minimum. Consistency beats optimization every time.
